About Me

Hometown

My hometown in Winchester, MA, was an interesting place to grow as a child into a young adult.

The Geographic location offered easy access to Boston, but also had seemingly endless forest to escape the city

Coming to Maine on vacations harbored a love for the state. With endless ways to spend time outside, and an attractive cost, coming to the University was a offer I could not refuse.

In high School I co-founded the Philosophy Club my third year. It was a great experience discussing ideas and polotics that where not shared in the classroom. Many of the members had conflicting political views, but through discussion, we all learned to be more open minded about the beliefs of each member.

I drive a 2007 Subaru Outback, with a standard transmission.

Although I do not obsess over cars, I enjoy the raw experience of driving a standard car.
